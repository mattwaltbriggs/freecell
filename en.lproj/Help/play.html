Freecell help

How do I play?

Making a move is easy. Click on the position you want to move from, and it will be highlighted. Then click on the destination, and the move will be made (if it is within the rules). If you make a mistake, you can always undo as many moves as you want.

Here, we're going to move the eight of hearts onto the nine of spades. The third column has been clicked, and the bottom card highlighted.
Now the second column has been clicked, and the card has been moved. We could also have moved the card to any of the four free cells above by clicking there instead.
